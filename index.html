How To Take Care Of Your Chihuahua Puppy

These are the 4 steps you need to take into account in order to properly take care of your puppy:

  1. Feeding your puppy healthy food
  2. Exercise your puppy daily
  3. Register your puppy and visit the veterinarian
  4. Have grooming sessions every other month
